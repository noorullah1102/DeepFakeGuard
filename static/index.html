What is DeepFakeGuard?

Traditional deepfake detectors give you a simple "real" or "fake" verdict. DeepFakeGuard goes further — it explains why media might be AI-generated using Claude AI, making threat intelligence accessible to non-technical teams and security analysts.

How It Works

1
File Upload & Validation
Audio or image is uploaded and validated for supported formats.
2
ML Ensemble + Rule-Based Analysis
Dual ML models (CommunityForensics ViT + prithivMLmods SigLIP) combine with OpenCV feature extraction for a weighted verdict.
3
AI-Powered Explanation
Claude AI generates a plain-English explanation of the threat, including severity and recommended action.
